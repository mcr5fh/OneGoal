About One Goal

OneGoal is a philanthropic organization founded at the University of Virginia that works to promote healthy, athletic behavior by collecting used sporting equipment and raising money to send that gear to impoverished areas both nationally and internationally. In the past we've worked largely with villages in South Africa but we are expanding our outreach to work in other areas such as Haiti, Nicaragua, Brazil, Argentina, and South Dakota.

Recent projects:

South Africa

The group that we sent equipment to in South Africa is a student run group from the University of Venda called Amplifying Community Voices. They used the equipment to help kick start a soccer league among the local communities. Through trans-disciplinary teams including faculty, students and community representatives, projects aim to address and improve a number of issues ranging from education, renewable energy, and numerous other areas that these communities would benefit from.

Haiti

St. Michel's Catholic School is a grade school located in Saltedere, Haiti that serves students ranging from pre-school to grade 13. St. Michel's is part of the Richmond and Hinche diocese ministry partnership, which aims to expand solidarity between the two communities. The sports gear donated will be going to the students at St. Michel's as well as three other local schools to be used by students during their recreational events.

Nicaragua

The Building Goodness Foundation is a nonprofit that connects skilled volunteers from the design and construction industries with vulnerable communities at home and abroad. In partnership with non-government organizations and domestic nonprofits, we build and renovate critically needed schools, clinics, homes, and centers for ecological and economic development.

Brazil

Seeds of Hope Brazil is a student run organization dedicated to combatting the social and economic injustices faced everyday by the children of Sao Paulo, Brazil. During spring break, students work to aid a local orphanage of the area, help with the construction of a trade-school for abandoned children in urban environments, and to love those who need it the most. Above all, Seeds of Hope Brazil wishes to create global understanding and long-lasting relationship of love with the underprivileged children in the Sao Paulo community.
